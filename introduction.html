About Us

Happening is an event-production company that puts together e-sport expositions, international tournaments, and live pro league events. They are focused on promoting both their events and their services as second-to-none for a wide variety of brands and clients.

Our Design System

Lively Design System encompasses that “in-the-moment” feeling: energetic, alive, and happy. Clients don’t have to wait for their event to experience a lively atmosphere; Lively Design System provides it from the moment you meet the Happening brand.
